# Ad Format Specifications

Technical specs for designing LinkedIn ad creatives. Adhering to these prevents
platform rejection and ensures maximum visibility across mobile/desktop.

## Single Image Ads

| Aspect Ratio | Resolution | Safe Zone | Max File Size | Recommended For |
|--------------|------------|-----------|---------------|-----------------|
| 1:1 (Square) | 1200 x 1200| Center 80%| 5 MB (JPG/PNG)| Feed (Desktop & Mobile) |
| 4:5 (Vertical)| 1080 x 1350| Center 75%| 5 MB (JPG/PNG)| Mobile Feed (Max coverage) |
| 1.91:1 (Horiz)| 1200 x 627 | Center 90%| 5 MB (JPG/PNG)| Right Rail (Desktop only) |

*Best Practice: Avoid 1.91:1 for feed placement as it occupies the least vertical space.*

## Video Ads

| Aspect Ratio | Duration | Max Size | Captions | Sound |
|--------------|----------|----------|----------|-------|
| 1:1 or 4:5 | 15s - 45s| 200 MB | Required (SRT or Burned) | Optional (assume muted) |

*Best Practice: The core value proposition or "hook" must appear on screen within the first 3 seconds, visually.*

## Document Ads

| Format | File Size | Max Pages | Ratio | Recommended For |
|--------|-----------|-----------|-------|-----------------|
| PDF, PPT, DOC| 100 MB | 300 | 4:5 or 1:1| Checklists, Playbooks, Case Studies |

*Best Practice: Under 10 pages performs best. The first page (cover) acts as the ad visual. Make it bold and click-worthy.*

## Carousel Image Ads

| Image Cards | Resolution | Max Size | Link Destination |
|-------------|------------|----------|------------------|
| 2 - 10 | 1080 x 1080 (1:1) | 10 MB per image | Unique URL per card allowed |

*Best Practice: The first card must act as an explicit hook prompting the user to swipe to the second card. Number the cards (e.g., "1/5").*
