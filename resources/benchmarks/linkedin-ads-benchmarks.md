# LinkedIn Ads Benchmarks

Use these benchmarks to evaluate campaign performance, set expectations,
and diagnose underperforming funnels. Data varies significantly by audience
seniority and geo, but these provide a baseline for North American B2B SaaS.

## Cost & Delivery Benchmarks (Overall)

| Metric | Healthy Average | Warning Zone | Critical Zone |
|--------|-----------------|--------------|---------------|
| Cost Per Click (CPC) | $6.00 - $12.00 | $15.00 - $20.00 | > $25.00 |
| Cost Per 1,000 Impressions (CPM)| $35 - $60 | $75 - $100 | > $120.00 |
| Click-Through Rate (CTR) | 0.45% - 0.70% | 0.30% - 0.40% | < 0.25% |

*Note: Demographics (e.g. C-Suite) will skew CPCs to the higher end ($15+).*

## Funnel Velocity Benchmarks

Tracking the backend conversion of LinkedIn generated leads.

| Funnel Stage | Conversion Rate Benchmark |
|--------------|---------------------------|
| Lead to MQL (Marketing Qualified) | 30% - 40% (Depends on ICP filter) |
| MQL to SQL (Sales Qualified/Meeting)| 5% - 10% (Content), 30%+ (Demo) |
| SQL to Closed Won | 15% - 25% |

## Audience Size Guidelines

| Campaign Type | Minimum Size | Optimal Range |
|---------------|--------------|---------------|
| Cold / Awareness | 20,000 | 50,000 - 100,000|
| Matched ABM List | 2,000 | 10,000+ |
| Website Retargeting | 300 | 1,000+ |

*Warning: Campaigns running on audiences <20k will experience rapid ad fatigue. Update creatives twice as frequently.*
