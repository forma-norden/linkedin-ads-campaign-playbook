# linkedin-ads-funnel-architect

Use this skill to design a multi-layer LinkedIn Ads funnel tailored to the
buyer's journey.

## Funnel Architecture

### Layer 1: Cold (Awareness/Education)
**Goal:** Establish the problem, build trust, generate website traffic.
**Audience:** Broad ICP attributes or large ABM list (50k+ members).
**Offer:** Ungated frameworks, POV videos, industry benchmarks.
**KPIs:** Reach, frequency (2-3x/week), CTR, cost per engaged visit.

### Layer 2: Warm (Retargeting/Consideration)
**Goal:** Deepen the pain and show the solution mechanism.
**Audience:** Visited website (Insight Tag), engaged with Layer 1 ads, or company page visitors.
**Offer:** Case studies, product teardowns, ROI calculators.
**KPIs:** Retargeting CTR, high-intent page views (pricing/demo pages).

### Layer 3: Hot (Capture/Conversion)
**Goal:** Convert intent into scheduled meetings or signed up users.
**Audience:** High-frequency website visitors, pricing page visitors, 90% video viewers.
**Offer:** Book a demo, start a trial, interactive product tour.
**KPIs:** Cost per Lead (CPL), Cost per Scheduled Meeting (CPSM), Lead-to-Meeting conversion rate.

## Budget Split by Funnel Layer

| Layer | Budget Weight | Why |
|-------|---------------|-----|
| Cold | 50-60% | The largest audience pool requiring the most impressions. |
| Warm | 25-30% | Smaller pool, but needs higher frequency. |
| Hot | 10-20% | Smallest pool, focused entirely on direct response. |

## Pre-Launch Architecture Checklist

1. Define the core objective (Pipeline vs. Brand Awareness).
2. Establish the budget ceiling.
3. Define the conversion event and ensure tracking is active.
4. Verify audience sizes at the Cold layer support the budget (min 10k members).
5. Map specific creatives to specific funnel layers.

## Output Contract
When asked to build a funnel, provide:
- The 3-layer breakdown specific to the user's product
- Assigned ad formats per layer
- Recommended initial budget split
- Core KPIs to track for each specific stage
