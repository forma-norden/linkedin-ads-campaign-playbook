# linkedin-ads-optimization-troubleshooting

Use this skill to diagnose failing campaigns and apply corrective actions
to stabilize Cost Per Lead (CPL) and improve pipeline quality.

## The Troubleshooting Hierarchy

When a campaign is failing, look at metrics in this specific order:

### 1. Delivery (Is the budget spending?)
**Symptom:** Daily spend is < 20% of the budget.
**Diagnosis:**
- Audience is too small (< 10k). Expand targeting.
- Bid is too low. Increase manual CPC by 15-20%.
- Creatives were rejected by policy. Check status.

### 2. Engagement (Are people clicking?)
**Symptom:** Spend is high, but Click-Through Rate (CTR) is < 0.3%.
**Diagnosis:**
- The creative is weak or fatigued. Swap images/video immediately.
- The audience is completely misaligned with the offer. Check demographic reporting (who is actually seeing the ads?).
- The ad format is wrong for the objective.

### 3. Conversion (Are clicks becoming leads?)
**Symptom:** CTR is high (> 0.6%), but Conversion Rate is low (< 5%).
**Diagnosis:**
- The landing page doesn't match the ad promise ("clickbait").
- The form is too long (too many required fields).
- The page load speed on mobile is atrocious (most clicks are mobile).
- Action: Switch to native Lead Gen Forms if landing page is the bottleneck.

### 4. Quality (Are leads becoming pipeline?)
**Symptom:** CPL is great, but Sales rejects every lead.
**Diagnosis:**
- The offer attracts the wrong people (e.g., offering a generic Macbook giveaway instead of a B2B checklist).
- Audience Extension or LinkedIn Audience Network (LAN) is enabled. **ALWAYS turn off LAN for B2B.**
- Action: Review "Demographics" tab to see job titles of leads. Add specific job title exclusions to the campaign.

## The 4-Week Optimization Cycle

- **Week 1:** Adjust Bids & Pacing. Ensure the campaign is spending smoothly. Turn off obvious "loser" creatives after 1,000 impressions if CTR is abysmal.
- **Week 2:** Adjust Creatives. Consolidate budget into the top 2-3 performing ads.
- **Week 3:** Adjust Placements/Audiences. Review demographics and add necessary exclusions.
- **Week 4:** Adjust Offers. If funnel is healthy (clicks+conversions) but pipeline isn't forming, the core offer must change.

## Output Contract
When asked to troubleshoot, provide:
- The likely root cause based on the metrics provided.
- A 3-step immediate action plan to halt budget waste.
- A structural fix to prevent the issue from recurring.
