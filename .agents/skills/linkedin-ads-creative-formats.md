# linkedin-ads-creative-formats

Use this skill to select the highest-performing ad format for a specific
campaign goal and audience size.

## Format Selection Guide

### 1. Single Image Ads (The Workhorse)
- **Best For:** Direct response (Lead Gen Forms), clear value propositions, statistics.
- **Design Rule:** Text should take up less than 20% of the image. Use bold contrast (dark backgrounds with bright text or vice versa).
- **Format:** 1200x1200px (1:1 square) or 1080x1350px (4:5 vertical). Vertical takes up more screen real estate on mobile.

### 2. Video Ads (The Brand Builder)
- **Best For:** Explaining complex products, founder stories, customer testimonials.
- **Design Rule:** The first 3 seconds must hook the viewer with motion or a bold statement. Always include burned-in captions (80% of video is watched on mute).
- **Format:** Keep under 45 seconds for cold audiences. 1:1 or 4:5 aspect ratio.

### 3. Document Ads (The Educator)
- **Best For:** Distributing playbooks, cheat sheets, and deep-dive research natively in the feed.
- **Design Rule:** Make the cover slide extremely compelling. Ensure text is legible on mobile.
- **Format:** PDF format. Can be gated (Lead Gen Form to unlock full doc) or ungated. Ungated drives drastically lower CPL if retargeting is set up correctly.

### 4. Carousel Ads (The Storyteller)
- **Best For:** Step-by-step processes, showcasing multiple product features, or highlighting multiple case studies in one ad.
- **Design Rule:** The first card must act as a hook that forces the user to swipe.
- **Format:** 1080x1080px (Square).

### 5. Thought Leader Ads (The Trust Accelerator)
- **Best For:** Promoting employee or executive posts from their personal profiles.
- **Design Rule:** The post must already be natively engaging. You are amplifying organic success, not buying engagement for a bad post.
- **Format:** Promoted organic post (must have permission from the creator).

## Creative Refresh Cadence

Ad fatigue is the #1 reason for a spike in Cost Per Click.
- **Audience < 50k:** Refresh creatives every 3-4 weeks.
- **Audience > 100k:** Refresh creatives every 6-8 weeks.
- **Retargeting Audiences:** Refresh every 2-3 weeks (frequency builds up extremely fast on small lists).

## Output Contract
When advising on creatives, provide:
- The optimal format for the stated goal
- Specific design constraints or dimensions
- The psychological angle the visual needs to convey
- Refresh cadence recommendations based on the audience size
